    <div style="margin-top: 4rem;">
        <h3 style="color: var(--color-uw-red); font-size: 1.5rem; margin-bottom: 1.5rem; font-family: var(--font-display); text-align: center;">Research Overview</h3>
        
        <div style="max-width: 900px; margin: 0 auto;">
            <p style="font-size: 1rem; line-height: 1.8; color: var(--color-text-medium); margin-bottom: 2rem;">
                Our research focuses on the theoretical foundations of quantum computing, with particular emphasis on 
                superconducting quantum circuits and their applications. We combine analytical methods with numerical 
                simulations to understand and optimize quantum gate operations, decoherence processes, and error correction.
            </p>
            
            <div class="research-grid">
                <div class="research-card">
                    <h4 style="color: var(--color-uw-red); margin-bottom: 0.8rem;">Theoretical Methods</h4>
                    <ul style="font-size: 0.9rem; line-height: 1.7; color: var(--color-text-medium); padding-left: 1.5rem;">
                        <li>Circuit quantization and Hamiltonian analysis</li>
                        <li>Master equation approaches for open quantum systems</li>
                        <li>Perturbation theory and effective Hamiltonian methods</li>
                        <li>Numerical diagonalization and time evolution</li>
                    </ul>
                </div>

                <div class="research-card">
                    <h4 style="color: var(--color-uw-red); margin-bottom: 0.8rem;">Current Projects</h4>
                    <ul style="font-size: 0.9rem; line-height: 1.7; color: var(--color-text-medium); padding-left: 1.5rem;">
                        <li>Fluxonium qubit gate optimization</li>
                        <li>Quantum error correction for superconducting circuits</li>
                        <li>Noise characterization and mitigation</li>
                        <li>Hybrid quantum-classical algorithms</li>
                    </ul>
                </div>

                <div class="research-card">
                    <h4 style="color: var(--color-uw-red); margin-bottom: 0.8rem;">Collaborations</h4>
